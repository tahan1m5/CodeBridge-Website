The Artist

Based in the heart of the coast, Elena Thorne bridges the gap between raw nature and modern minimalism.

With over 15 years of experience in traditional crafts, Elena’s work is characterized by its monochrome palette, allowing texture and shadow to take center stage. Her pieces are held in private collections across Europe and North America.
